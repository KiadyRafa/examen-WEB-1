About Me

Social Innovator

I am currently a dedicated computer science student, specializing in data analysis and software development. My passion for technology and my constant thirst for learning drive me to explore new technologies and seek innovative ways to apply my skills. Alongside my academic training, I am deeply attached to my native country, Madagascar. I am aware of the socio-economic challenges we face and am determined to contribute to the improvement of our society. My goal is to use my computer skills to develop solutions that can help overcome these challenges. Whether it’s creating educational platforms to improve access to education, developing applications to connect farmers to markets, or using data analysis to solve environmental problems, I am motivated by the desire to make a difference. In short, I am a passionate computer science student and a devoted patriot, aspiring to use technology to improve the lives of people in Madagascar. I firmly believe that love of country and passion for technology can go hand in hand to create a better future.
